	DT228-3 User Interface Drafts Continuous Assessment 2009-10
	-----------------------------------------------------------
	SimpleDrafts
	------------


	Moving Pieces
	-------------
	For this Assignment i have the Draftpiece move by hold down the mouse on the piece and and dragging it off that 
	panel. Then to place that piece the user can simply click on an empty panel that is one ahead of the previous 
	panel. 

	Capturing Pieces
	----------------
	To Capture a Piece the user can click and drag a piece off a panel and click over an opposite piece. Then click
	and drag on the piece that was just placed and put it on the another panel.
